Using AppGyver Scanner

On iOS, to restart your current app or scan another app, tap and hold on the screen with three fingers.. This opens the Scanner context menu.

On Android, press the native back button to return to the device desktop. Open the Scanner again to scan another app or re-open your previous app.

Start the tutorial

Let's spend a few minutes on a short tutorial. Go to your Steroids project directory and run the command:

$ steroids generate tutorial begin
